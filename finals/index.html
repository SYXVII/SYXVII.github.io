What is Novel?

A novel is a literary work of fiction that weaves a unique narrative, introducing readers to new characters, settings, and experiences. It is distinguished by its creative storytelling, offering fresh perspectives on the human condition, society, or the world. Novels often explore complex themes, emotions, and relationships, providing readers with a rich and immersive journey through the imagination of the author.
