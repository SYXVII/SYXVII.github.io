Top 2 The Legendary Mechanic

By Qi Peijia

This has been a great experience with me. It had such a good plot, being the main character becoming a NPC (non playable character) in the game, while also interacting with other players, trying to post as an actual npc by giving mission to only use tham as cheap manual labor. Although there was a lot of things i skimmed because of how detailed some explanations are, this was such a great experience to me and i know this novel will be remembered for years.

Top 3 The Achievement Junkie by TheSilverQuill

The novel is all about a person getting transported inside the game after having 100% completion of all achievements. Basically, he completed the game with all the missions and all the things you can do the game had been done. Now with that depth of knowledge, he is now tasked to save the world again after the so called "players" disappeared for centuries and are all now myths.

Top 4 Chrysalis by RinoZ

It started with a perspective of someone who cannot see or feel anything, but they know their body, their spirit is being molded into something else. After an unknown amount of time, he opened his eyes for the first and last time. He was now “alive” but not as a human, but as an ant. It’s about a story about jolly ANThony trying to survive as an ant. This simple like novel is not as simple as I thought it would, specially the great reveal how he died and the reason why he was revived as an ant. It’s a fun unique story that is I highly encourage you to read no matter what age.

Top 5 Overlord by Kugane Maruyama

The plot begins on the last day of Yggdrasil, a popular online game that is being quietly discontinued. Momonga, our protagonist, decides to stay in his favorite game until the very end, so he waits for the forced logout. Unexpectedly, the server does not shut down, and Momonga is trapped in another world as a skeleton avatar. "The powerful Overlord" must now explore the new world and face the ongoing challenges.

Top 6 That Time I Reincarnated as a Slime by Fuse

Top 7 Solo Leveling by Chugong

In a world where magical hunters battle monsters, Sung Jinwoo, a weak hunter, faces endless survival struggles. He is chosen by the System program after surviving a powerful dungeon, granting him rare strength levels. He fights enemies in order to discover dungeon secrets and his powers. I got hooked by the art style of manga but stayed for the novel, although now that the manga is finished I fell in love with both.

Top 8 The Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint by Sing Shong

Omniscient reader’s Viewpoint is a story about a story. A story that revolves and is fueled around multiple mini stories. I had trouble trying to explain this novel but I think that sums how good this novel is, keeping you hooked not expecting to happen because almost anything can happen.

Top 9 My Vampire System by JKSManga

The human race is fighting the Vicious Dalki, and hidden humans with special abilities emerge. Some people share their knowledge to win the war, while others keep their skills to themselves. Quinn, who has lost everything, receives a book and gains power through quests. However, he is confronted with a quest that he is unable to complete. It's about a young boy who is given something that will change his life forever.

Top 10 Free Fall by Guiltythree

Wraiths are genetically altered people with unique abilities. However, ability does not always imply power: it often simply makes you a target. This is especially true for Matthew, who may be one of the most powerful wraiths in the world - and thus must conceal his power and rely on cunning to survive. And then there's the girl who wants him to be a member of her rock band...
